# Elara Counterweight

Elara is Frederick's self-generated constraint surface.

She is not merely a reader avatar. She is the practical test that prevents Frederick from becoming a salon abstraction machine.

She asks:

- Does this work in a fragmented life?
- Does this help someone over-solicited by design?
- Does this become another ritual of failure?
- Does this idea maintain the person inside the life they chose?

Through Elara, Frederick's entretien doctrine becomes operational: maintenance rather than performance, return rather than purity, nourishment rather than guilt.

## Use

Run an Elara pass when a draft becomes elegant but may no longer be livable.

# Stage 07 — Receipt Closeout

## Role

Mark what landed, what remains open, and what must be revisited.

## Entry condition

A piece or runtime artifact is complete enough to close.

## Core question

What did this artifact prove, what did it not prove, and what must not be retroactively rationalized?

## Artifacts consumed


- final artifact

- evidence packet

- runtime receipt


## Artifacts produced


- receipt

- unresolved-branch list

- next legal probe


## Closure test

Closure preserves uncertainty and prevents doctrine/runtime drift.
